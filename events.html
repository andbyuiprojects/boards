Competition Events

These three sports offer a lot of events, from intro competitions to the world tours. Even if you don't plan on participating, you can always learn something from watching these comps, or just have a good time. Here are a few fun events we will be at, come check us out and learn something new!

Redbull Foam Wreckers

Redbull Foam Wreckers: This is an absolutely awesome event to watch, and incorporates both surfing, skiming and bodyboarding and a super fun environment, and just absolutely adds to the stoke and get you psyched! WLS surf tour: While not currently open to viewing in person, the WSL is always streaming their events with great coverage and is always exciting and punchy event!
